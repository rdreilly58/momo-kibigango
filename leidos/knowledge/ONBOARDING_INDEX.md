## Key Principles to Remember

### 1. Listen More Than You Talk
- Goal: 70% listening, 30% talking
- Your job this week is to understand, not to teach

### 2. No Announcements Yet
- This week is observation
- Next week you can suggest improvements
- Month 2-3 you'll make changes

### 3. Document Everything
- Good memory is great, written notes are better
- You'll need these baseline observations in month 2
- Patterns emerge from written notes, not mental notes

### 4. Be Curious, Not Judgmental
- If something seems broken, ask "why" first
- There's usually a good reason for how things are
- Avoid "we need to change X" language

### 5. Build Relationships First
- People work for people they trust
- Earning trust now pays dividends for months
- Take genuine interest in their success

## What NOT to Do

❌ Don't announce changes or vision day 1  
❌ Don't criticize previous leadership  
❌ Don't make promises you're not sure you can keep  
❌ Don't skip any 1:1s (these are critical)  
❌ Don't spend all day in email (people first)  
❌ Don't pick sides in any conflicts (stay neutral)  
❌ Don't go silent (visibility matters)

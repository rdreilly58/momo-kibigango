## Key Principles to Remember

### 🎯 This Week Is About...
**Listening, not leading. Observing, not changing. Building trust, not proving yourself.**

### 📝 Do This
- ✅ Listen 70%, talk 30%
- ✅ Ask thoughtful questions
- ✅ Take detailed notes
- ✅ Show genuine curiosity about people and context

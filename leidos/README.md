## 🚀 Key Principles

**Listen First, Lead Later**
- Week 1: Observation mode (70% listen, 30% talk)
- Week 2-3: Deepen understanding, propose improvements
- Week 4+: Begin implementation with team alignment

**Build Relationships First**
- People work for leaders they trust
- Earning trust requires time, consistency, respect
- This week is about trust foundation

**Document Everything**
- Good memory is good, written notes are better
- Patterns emerge from documentation
- You'll need baseline observations to measure progress

**No Announcements Yet**
- This week is learning
- Avoid saying "we need to change X"
- Build consensus before proposing changes

## 📊 What You're Doing This Week

**Listening Tour** (15+ structured conversations)
- 1:1s with direct reports (engineering managers)
- 1:1s with senior individual contributors
- Cross-functional conversations (Product, Ops, Security, QA, Customer Success)
- Deep dives (Jira/metrics, architecture, security/compliance)

**Learning Goals** (what you'll understand by Friday)
- People structure: Who are they? What do they do? Who are the strong performers?
- Technical baseline: DORA metrics, architecture, testing, CI/CD, technical debt
- Pain points: What's hard? What's frustrating? What's limiting us?
- Quick wins: What can you fix quickly to build momentum?
- Security context: What compliance and security constraints shape decisions?

**Relationship Goals** (trust foundation)
- Do people feel heard? (you listened)
- Do people respect you? (you asked smart questions, acknowledged expertise)
- Do people trust you? (you followed up, kept confidences, were consistent)

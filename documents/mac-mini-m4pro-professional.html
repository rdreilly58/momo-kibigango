AWS EC2 vs. Home Purchase: Total Cost of Ownership Comparison

Executive Summary

This comprehensive analysis evaluates the financial feasibility of purchasing an Apple Mac mini M4 Pro with 48GB RAM and 1TB storage versus utilizing AWS EC2 mac-m4pro.metal instances for development and computational workloads.

Key Finding: Home purchase becomes cost-effective after 18 months of continuous use; AWS is preferable for short-term or variable workloads.

Recommendations

Choose AWS EC2 if:

  • Your workload is sporadic or bursty (not continuous)
  • You need flexibility to rapidly scale resources up or down
  • Your project duration is anticipated under 12 months
  • You prefer to avoid hardware maintenance responsibilities
  • You want to minimize upfront capital investment

Choose Home Purchase if:

  • You require continuous or near-continuous access for 18+ months
  • You want complete control and ownership of your hardware
  • You can integrate it into your home or office infrastructure
  • You view it as a long-term productivity investment
  • You prefer to eliminate recurring monthly cloud expenses

Additional Considerations

AWS EC2 Advantages:

  • On-demand scalability for resource optimization
  • No hardware maintenance or repair responsibilities
  • Access to global AWS infrastructure and services
  • Seamless integration with other AWS cloud services

Home Purchase Advantages:

  • Complete hardware ownership with no recurring fees
  • Zero marginal cost after initial purchase
  • Immediate local access without network latency
  • Strong resale value on secondary market

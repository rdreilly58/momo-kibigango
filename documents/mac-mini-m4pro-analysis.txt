EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This analysis evaluates the financial feasibility of purchasing an Apple Mac 
mini M4 Pro (48GB RAM, 1TB storage) versus utilizing AWS EC2 mac-m4pro.metal 
instances for development and computational workloads.

KEY FINDING: Home purchase becomes cost-effective after 18 months of 
continuous use; AWS is preferable for short-term or variable workloads.

BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS
================================================================================

Break-even point at continuous usage: 18 MONTHS

After 18 months, the home purchase has paid for itself through reduced AWS 
fees. Beyond this point, home ownership becomes exponentially more economical.

================================================================================
RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

CHOOSE AWS EC2 IF:
  ✓ Your workload is sporadic or bursty (not continuous)
  ✓ You need flexibility to scale resources up/down
  ✓ Your project duration is under 12 months
  ✓ You prefer no hardware maintenance burden
  ✓ You want to minimize upfront capital investment

CHOOSE HOME PURCHASE IF:
  ✓ You need continuous or near-continuous access (18+ months)
  ✓ You want complete control over your hardware
  ✓ You can integrate it into your home/office setup
  ✓ Long-term productivity tools investment
  ✓ You want to avoid recurring monthly cloud bills

================================================================================
ADDITIONAL CONSIDERATIONS
================================================================================

AWS EC2 ADVANTAGES:
  • Scale resources on demand
  • No hardware maintenance responsibilities
  • Global AWS infrastructure and services
  • Easy integration with other AWS services

HOME PURCHASE ADVANTAGES:
  • Full ownership with no recurring fees
  • Zero marginal cost after initial purchase
  • Immediate local access (no cloud latency)
  • Excellent resale value on secondary market

================================================================================
CONCLUSION
================================================================================

The choice between AWS EC2 and home purchase depends on your usage pattern:

• Short-term projects (< 12 months): AWS offers lower total cost and better 
  flexibility

• Long-term development (18+ months): Home purchase provides 92%+ cost 
  savings and permanent asset ownership

• Variable/burst workloads: AWS provides better cost efficiency through 
  pay-as-you-go pricing

• Continuous workloads: Home purchase is significantly more economical

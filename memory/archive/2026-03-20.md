**Pass 2:** Typography & icon reduction

- Feature icons: `w-6 h-6` → `w-3 h-3`
- Small SVGs: `w-4 h-4` → `w-2 h-2`
- Emojis: `text-2xl` → `text-lg`
- Emoji margins: `mb-4` → `mb-2`

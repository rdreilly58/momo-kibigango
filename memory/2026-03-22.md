### Strategy Framework Created

**File:** `leidos/knowledge/LEADERSHIP_STRATEGY.md` (comprehensive guide)

**Four Core Pillars (aligned to job description):**

1. **Organizational & Technical Leadership**
   - Define long-term technical strategy via RFC process
   - Domain-based architecture (multiple domain leads)
   - Engineering excellence (CI/CD, testing, security, reliability)
   - Responsible AI adoption with governance framework

2. **People Management & Leadership Development**
   - Distributed leadership (don't be a bottleneck)
   - Clear career paths (IC track vs. management track)
   - Succession planning (identify 2-3 candidates per critical role)
   - Continuous feedback (weekly 1:1s, quarterly reviews)

3. **Delivery & Execution**
   - DORA metrics (deployment frequency, lead time, failure rate, recovery time)
   - Jira workflows with flow insights
   - Sprint planning balance (60% delivery / 40% platform/debt)
   - Cross-functional collaboration (product, stakeholders, customers)

4. **Responsible AI Governance** (explicit mandate in job description)
   - "Responsible AI by Design" framework (policy → checkpoints → training → monitoring)
   - Four governance layers: Policy, Checkpoints, Training, Monitoring
   - Competitive advantage: Upskilled teams move faster, early compliance

### 90-Day Implementation Roadmap
- **Week 1:** Discovery & listening (meet team, learn context)

### 2026 Best Practices Researched & Integrated

- **Effective Leadership:** Shared leadership, team orientation, redundancy, adaptability, peer feedback
- **AI Governance:** World Economic Forum framework (Feb 2026) + Zibtek AI governance
- **Responsible AI by Design:** Build governance into SDLC, not after deployment
- **DORA Metrics:** Industry standard for measuring engineering health

### Local LLM Training Research (Deep Dive)

**Question:** How is model training done for local LLMs?

**Three-phase overview researched:**
1. **Pretraining** — From scratch on raw text ($100k-$1M, days on GPU clusters) — Not practical locally
2. **Fine-Tuning** — Adapt existing models to your domain:
   - Full fine-tune: Hours on GPU, 10K+ examples, high quality
   - **LoRA (Local):** Minutes-hours on M4, 100-1K examples, 80-85% quality
   - Prompt engineering: Free, no training, limited
3. **RLHF** — Ranking-based optimization (expensive, skip for local)

**LoRA Sweet Spot for M4:**
- Train: 4-8 hours on your Mac (overnight)
- Adapter size: 50-100 MB (tiny, shareable)
- Cost: $0 (electricity ~$3)
- Quality: 80-85% (excellent for domain-specific)
- Model: Qwen 14B quantized (fits M4 memory)

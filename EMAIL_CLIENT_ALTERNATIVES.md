## Migration Path

### **From Spark to Apple Mail**
1. **Backup:** Export contacts & settings from Spark
2. **Setup:** Add email accounts to Mail (System Settings → Internet Accounts)
3. **Rules:** Create Smart Mailboxes for organization
4. **Contacts:** Import contacts if needed
5. **Time:** 30 minutes total
6. **Risk:** Very low (all data stays in Gmail)

### **From Spark to Canary Mail**
1. **Install:** Download from Mac App Store
2. **Setup:** Add email accounts (same auth as Spark)
3. **Sync:** iPhone automatically syncs settings
4. **Time:** 15 minutes total
5. **Cost:** $20/year
6. **Risk:** Very low (free trial available)

### **From Spark to Mimestream**
1. **Install:** Download from website
2. **Auth:** Connect to Gmail (OAuth)
3. **Configure:** Minimal setup needed
4. **Time:** 10 minutes total
5. **iPhone:** Continue using native Mail app
6. **Risk:** Very low (read-only on Gmail)

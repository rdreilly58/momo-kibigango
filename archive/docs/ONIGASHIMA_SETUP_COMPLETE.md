## 🎯 Project Overview

### Name & Branding
- **Project:** Onigashima (鬼ヶ島 — "Demon Island" from Momotaro folklore)
- **Tagline:** "Your Personal AI That Actually Knows You"
- **Positioning:** Privacy-first alternative to ChatGPT with complete system

### Three-Tier Product Strategy

**Tier 1: Self-Install Software**
- Price: Free → $99/year (freemium)
- Target: DIY users, developers, enthusiasts
- Timeline: Launch Month 4 (beta)

**Tier 2: Hardware**
- Price: $899-$1,299
- Includes: Pre-configured Mac mini + 1-year support
- Target: Non-technical consumers, small business
- Timeline: Hardware pilot Month 6, public launch Month 7

**Tier 3: Cloud Services**
- Price: Included or $5/month
- Includes: Updates, backups, analytics
- Target: All users
- Timeline: Launch with Phase 1

### Financial Projections

**Year 1:**
- Revenue: $250K-$1.2M
- EBITDA: -$100K to +$200K
- Users: 1K-5K self-install, 200-1K hardware

- Revenue: $2.5M-$12M

- Users: 15K-60K self-install, 3K-15K hardware

- Revenue: $12M-$60M
